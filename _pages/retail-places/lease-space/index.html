Stores

Stores are the front lines of retail where personal interactions and customer experiences reign. The opportunity to engage your customer in a physical space creates an unparalleled connection with your brand. Let us help accomplish your store opening goals at local, regional and national levels. We’re excited to talk to new-to-market and existing concepts alike.

Entertainment

You specialize in bringing a unique experience to customers. Centennial will help you create a winning environment. Whether it’s bowling, movies, skydiving or indoor surfing, they all have one thing in common; creating a shared, memorable experience. Let Centennial be a gateway to new markets with your experiential concept.

Restaurants

Centennial believes that food creates a natural gathering place. And variety brings a community together. National favorites, farm-to-table, home-style ethnic food, and chef-driven concepts, all bring unique flavors to our restaurant mix. Whether you’re a local, regional or national operator let’s start a conversation to bring your cuisine to life.

Eateries

We know small footprint eateries can make a big impact on customer taste buds. Our portfolio welcomes creative and specialty food uses with flexible small space options to fit your bite- sized business layouts.

Fitness

The focus on health and wellness isn’t just a millennial trend, it’s a way of life for all ages. Our centers draw a diverse demographic of millions of shoppers a year, so whether 2,000 s. f. or 20,000 s. f. is right-sized for your yoga, barre, HIIT or health club, Centennial can provide the space and visibility you’re looking for.

Grocery

Although not a traditional offering at regional shopping centers, Centennial looks at these partnerships from a modern perspective. Tomorrow’s consumers are looking for convenience and we offer the parking, easy access, and a variety of cross-shopping opportunities ideal for these shoppers. Together, we can create a one-stop-shop they’ll come back for again, and again.

Co-Working

Our forward-thinking approach is a perfect complement to Co-working partners. We understand how sharing a network and a common space can accelerate ideas. Our centers afford the opportunity to creating stunning workspaces where professionals can join hundreds of social employees at a community hub while enjoying multitudes of lunchtime and after work happy hour options.

Public Use

Public uses are vital to our communities, and we want our properties to be the hub of each and every one. Centennial shopping centers are centrally located off major thoroughfares in each of their markets, providing some of the most accessible real estate available. Where better for a library, police station or school extension? We are happy to work with our municipal partners to make our neighborhoods better for all community members.
