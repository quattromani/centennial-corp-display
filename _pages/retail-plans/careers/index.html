Work/Life Balance

Work should be gratifying but we all need time away to recharge, travel, take care of personal matters, or spend time with family and friends. Centennial offers a generous PTO (paid time off) policy, volunteer programs and corporate flex hours—there’s no one-size solution for helping you achieve your best.

Comprehensive Benefits

In addition to competitive salaries, we offer an array of comprehensive benefits including healthcare, dental and vision insurance, flex spending accounts, and 401K matching. With quality benefits from premium providers, we’re committed to making life better for employees and their families.

Office Culture

Our modern, open concept corporate office sets the tone for accessible and collaborative work relationships. Casual Fridays, team spirit events, and holiday parties are just a few examples of how we create an environment that promotes fun in the workplace, and triggers a passion for what we do.
