DALLAS – February 16, 2015– Centennial Real Estate Company, a real estate firm specializing in the investment, development and management of retail properties, recently hired Nick Bendorf as assistant vice president on the acquisitions team. Bendorf is responsible for underwriting acquisition opportunities, as well as managing the due diligence and closing processes.

Prior to joining Centennial, Bendorf served as an associate at Hudson Advisors, the real estate arm of Lone Star Funds. At Hudson, he was responsible for the asset management of over 3.2 million square feet of retail space with a market value of over $400 million. His transactional experience at Hudson included acquiring over $300 million in assets and selling over $90 million. Bendorf also was responsible for supporting the management and coordination of a $1.8 billion multifamily portfolio. In that capacity, he helped establish the value-add strategy for renovating over 18,000 apartment units across the country, as well as developed and maintained the portfolio’s financial model. His previous experience includes roles at a publicly traded REIT, Digital Realty Trust (DLR) and a commercial appraisal firm, Integra.

Bendorf holds a master’s degree in real estate and a bachelor’s degree in finance from Texas A&M University. He is a member of The Real Estate Council and lives in the Deep Ellum neighborhood of Dallas.
